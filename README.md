# Thesis Repository / 论文仓库

## English

Welcome to my thesis repository! This repository serves as a platform for sharing my latest observations and perspectives on various political-economic and social issues.

### Purpose

This repository contains academic papers and analytical essays that explore contemporary challenges and transformations in our global society. Topics include:

- Political economy and welfare systems
- Global supply chains and trust mechanisms
- Energy transitions and employment structures
- Economic transformations and structural changes

### Content

The papers in this repository represent ongoing research and critical analysis. I will update this repository periodically as new insights emerge and new perspectives develop.

### Engagement

I welcome comments, discussions, and constructive feedback. Feel free to open issues or discussions to share your thoughts on any of the papers or topics covered here.

## 中文

欢迎来到我的论文仓库！本仓库旨在分享我对各种政治经济和社会问题的最新观察与观点。

### 主要用途

本仓库包含学术论文和分析文章，探讨当代全球社会面临的挑战与转型。主题包括：

- 政治经济学与福利制度
- 全球供应链与信任机制
- 能源转型与就业结构
- 经济转型与结构变化

### 内容说明

本仓库中的论文代表持续的研究和批判性分析。随着新见解的出现和新观点的发展，我会不定期更新本仓库。

### 互动交流

欢迎大家评论、讨论和建设性的反馈。您可以通过提交 issues 或参与讨论来分享对这些论文或主题的想法。
